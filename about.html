About Us

The main purpose of this website is to provide a platform for the young students still studying in secondary school or in colleges that have an interest in the field of computers but do not possess the acquired knowledge and skills needed. This site consists of all the fundamental courses that would help them to broaden their level of thinking, of problem solving and provide them with the necessary knowledge and skill needed for them to jump-start their careers. The teachers that will be conducting the lectures will be graduated employees with minimum bachelors degree in the computer field.
